gluckschwein

These are lucky pigs! In Germany pigs are a sign of good luck, and marzipan piglets are often shared amongst friends and family over the New Year to bring good luck over the next 12 months. And our pigs are double good luck, as they each have a second good luck charm, or “glucksbringer” ‐ a four‐leaf clover, a ladybug, a horseshoe, a toadstool, or a gold coin, painted in edible gold!

Available in sets of 5 or 10.
