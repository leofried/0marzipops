box of candy corn

Our latest creation ‐ marzipan bites! In each box you will find delicious bite‐size tri‐color pieces of marzipan that resemble candy corn. Larger than traditional candy corn at about 3/4‐to‐1 inch wide in size, this is grown up candy corn that is notable for what it does not include: no corn syrup, no gelatin, no caruba wax and no confectioners glaze.

Sold in a box by the dozen, the bites are perfect for gift giving and playful eating!

Available in gift boxes of 24, 36 or 48 pieces.
