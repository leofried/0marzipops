cameos

We are in love with these stunning floral marzipan cameos!

The word ‘cameo’ comes from ‘Kameo’, a word used in kabbalistic slang to signify a “magical square”, or talisman upon which magical spells are carved. We like to think these floral cameos will tickle your taste buds, like magic!

Order these marzipops with or without the sticks.

Available in sets of 4 or 8.
